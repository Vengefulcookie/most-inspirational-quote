"It's not impossible, it's just hard"
- Kōtarō Bokuto

Just because things seem bigger than us, that does not mean they will best us. We tend to forget that our goals were never meant to be impossible, just that they needed for us to put our most if we really want to see the fruit of our labour. So this simple quote reminds me that whatever may be thrown my way, all I have to do is keep trying because it's not impossible...it's just hard.
